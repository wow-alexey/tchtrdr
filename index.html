FAQ

Why should i buy BitMille ?
Unlike other tokens that promise to solve a future and unreliable paradigm, BitMille will kickoff as a token you can trade and use within a real life application (TouchTrader) very soon. Also, as we integrate our production version with other exchanges, BitMille footprint, liquidability and price can only increase.
Will BitMille have any other use ?
Yes absolutely. We have a vision in deliver a suite of disruptive products and BitMille will always be the token to use within our growing ecosystem.
Which wallet should i use to get BitMilles?
Anyone that purchases the token can easily and manage their BitMilles using existing Ethereum clients including the official Ethereum wallet, MetaMask, MyEtherWallet.com , etc. Since BitMille is a ERC-20 standard token it can leverage all the resources standing on top of the Ethereum ecosystem.
Is BitMille instantly transferable or there is some sort of lockup period for them?
BitMille does not have a lockup period and will be instantly transferable as soon as the ICO ends. The decision to hold it and see how our company expands or transfer it will be in your hands.
Why choose the Touchtrader app if i can go to any exchange do trading there?
TouchTrader can work as a rich standalone interface for trading or a complement for a user’s experience within an exchange. We focus on delivering an unique fun game like experience which distinguishes us from regular gray standard financial interfaces. And we have creative talented people to make sure we will be continuously flushing out the best user experiences for trading and beyond.
Where does the money go if i buy your BitMille?
Our company is tech focused so there will be an emphasis in putting out the best resources to our dev and creative team to build an array of world class trading experiences. Besides that the expenses incide generally on:
  • Expanding our company resources and infrastructure
  • Sales & Marketing section
  • R&D for other products we wish to launch
  • Business Development
  • Legal, Logistics & Accounting
What’s the vision of TouchTrader.io?
Our short and mid term goals are to disrupt the way we interact with financial data by putting the use of agile and creative practices in implementing great innovative user experiences. From a long term perspective we want to contribute to the digital economy landscape in a meaningful way.
Will the Touchtrader app be your only product?
Absolutely not. TouchTrader is our first chapter into building a great array of products. We aim to be a company ahead of its game and use sheer talent and hard work to make a dent in the financial and crypto ecosystem.
